## 4. Description of the Platform

NeuraLaunch is an AI-powered platform that provides startup validation, strategic recommendation, execution planning, and ongoing advisory support. The Platform uses artificial intelligence agents powered by third-party language model providers (including Anthropic and Google) to deliver its services.

### 4.1 Core Services

The Platform provides the following services, subject to your subscription tier:

**Discovery Interview:** An AI-conducted interview that gathers context about your situation, goals, skills, constraints, and aspirations to build a comprehensive understanding of your circumstances.

**Recommendation:** A single, committed strategic recommendation with supporting reasoning, assumptions, risks, and rejected alternatives, generated through AI synthesis of your interview data.

**Pushback System:** An adversarial dialogue system (up to 7 rounds) where you can challenge, question, or disagree with the recommendation and the AI will defend, refine, or replace its recommendation based on the merits of your argument.

**Execution Roadmap:** A phased, task-level execution plan tailored to your available time, resources, and constraints.

**Internal Execution Tools:**
- **Conversation Coach:** AI-powered preparation and rehearsal for high-stakes business conversations, including script generation, objection handling, and role-play simulation.
- **Outreach Composer:** AI-generated outreach messages for WhatsApp, email, and LinkedIn in single, batch, and sequence modes.
- **Research Tool:** Deep, multi-source market research with cited findings, contact information, and actionable insights.
- **Service Packager:** AI-generated service packaging with tiered pricing, revenue scenarios, and shareable service briefs.

**Check-in System:** Per-task progress tracking with AI-powered responses, pattern detection, and recalibration offers when execution evidence suggests a change in direction may be warranted.

**Continuation System:** End-of-cycle interpretation of execution outcomes, including a structured brief that assesses what happened, what assumptions were wrong, what the evidence suggests, and what the recommended next directions are.

**Voice Mode:** Voice-enabled interaction with the Platform's AI agents, including voice-conducted interviews, voice check-ins, and voice-enabled tool interactions. Voice data is transcribed, processed, and stored as text alongside the voice session.

**Cross-Session Memory:** Persistent memory across multiple discovery and execution cycles, allowing the Platform's AI agents to reference your prior execution history, outcomes, and learnings when providing new recommendations and guidance.

**Validation Landing Page:** For software-oriented recommendations, a live, deployed landing page with analytics, email capture, and feature interest tracking to validate market demand before development.

### 4.2 AI-Powered Nature of Services

All recommendations, roadmaps, scripts, messages, research reports, pricing packages, and other outputs generated by the Platform are produced by artificial intelligence systems. While we strive to deliver high-quality, contextually relevant outputs, the Platform does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or suitability of any AI-generated content for your specific circumstances.

The Platform does not provide licensed professional advice in any regulated field, including but not limited to legal advice, financial advice, investment advice, tax advice, medical advice, or accounting services.
